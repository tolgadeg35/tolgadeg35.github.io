About US


We're a rookie (in word) FRC team, newly founded and supported by three bright undergraduate students which are studying in the greatest universities in Turkey such as Boğaziçi University and METU. Our thing in common is the school we have studied and studying still. uSELESScASE is İzmir Bornova Anatolian High School's FRC team. Our aim is to give opportunity to all of the students in our school. We believe we will be the team you are looking for when you feel the inevitable desire for doing something beneficial for everybody in every way.
